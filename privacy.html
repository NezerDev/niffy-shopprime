Introduction

Niffy Shopprime ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect when you browse and shop with us, why we collect it, and how it's kept safe.

Information We Collect

  • Order details — when you check out, your name, phone number, and order items are sent to us via WhatsApp, and a record of the order is kept for our own reference.
  • Shopping bag — the items in your bag are stored only in your own browser (using a technology called "local storage"), not on our servers, until you check out.
  • Anonymous visit counts — we record how many times the site is visited, for our own understanding of traffic. This is a simple counter and is not tied to your name or identity.

How We Use Your Information

We use your order details only to fulfil and communicate about your order with you on WhatsApp. We use visit counts only to understand how the store is doing and where to improve it.

No Online Payments

This website does not process payments. All payment is arranged directly with us over WhatsApp once your order is confirmed. We never collect or store your card or bank details on this site.

Cookies

This site does not use cookies. It uses your browser's local storage only to remember your shopping bag and speed up browsing on repeat visits. That information stays on your device — we don't have access to it, and it isn't shared with anyone.

Data Storage & Security

Order and product information is stored securely using Firebase, a Google Cloud service. Access to the store's management dashboard is protected by a login and limited to authorised staff only.

Your Rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023, you have the right to ask what information we hold about you, request that we correct or delete it, and ask questions about how it's used. To do so, contact us using the details below.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Children

This site is not directed at children, and we don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
